## 🤖 About Me

I'm **Muhammed Zain**, a Robotics and Autonomous Systems Engineer passionate about building intelligent autonomous ground vehicles (UGVs) and developing robust perception, localization, and control systems for real-world applications.

- 🚀 Currently working as **Preventure Lead** at **ArtPark – AI & Robotics Technology Park (I-Hub), IISc Bangalore**, leading the development of autonomous off-road ground vehicles and intelligent robotic platforms.
- 🧠 Previously worked as a **Project Associate** at the **AI & Robotics Lab, Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore**, contributing to autonomous navigation, perception, and robotics research.
- 🤖 Experienced in developing complete autonomous robotic systems—from hardware integration and sensor calibration to software deployment and field validation.
- 🛰️ Research interests include **LiDAR Odometry**, **Visual-LiDAR Fusion**, **Multi-Sensor Calibration**, **Localization**, **SLAM**, **Sensor Fusion**, and **Autonomous Navigation**.
- ⚙️ Specialized in **robotics controls**, **drive-by-wire systems**, **embedded systems**, **ROS/ROS 2**, and autonomous software stack integration.
- 🚙 Currently working on **autonomous off-road UGVs**, focusing on perception, localization, path planning, motion control, and real-time vehicle deployment.
- 📡 Experienced with **GNSS/RTK**, **IMUs**, **LiDARs**, **RGB & Thermal Cameras**, **Radar**, and edge AI computing platforms.
- 💻 Passionate about building scalable robotic software using **C++**, **Python**, **ROS**, **Docker**, and modern robotics frameworks.
- 🔬 Interested in benchmarking SLAM and localization algorithms for GPS-denied environments and challenging off-road terrains.
- 🤝 Always excited to collaborate on robotics, autonomous vehicles,sensor fusion, and open-source robotics projects.
- 🎓 Bachelor's degree in **Electrical and Electronics Engineering**, with a strong focus on robotics, autonomous systems, embedded electronics, and control engineering.

- **Autonomous Navigation for a 6×6 Off-Road Vehicle** — Designed and implemented localization, path planning, and autonomous navigation for GPS-available and GPS-denied off-road environments using ROS-based software.
- **Multi-Sensor Calibration** — Performed intrinsic and extrinsic calibration of LWIR, RGB, and LiDAR sensors for accurate multimodal perception and sensor fusion.
